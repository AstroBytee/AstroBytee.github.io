Website Evaluations

Arngren

Arngren is a online marketplace that sells a wide variety of products, including electronics, toys, tools, and household items. The website is known for its cluttered and overwhelming design, which can make it difficult for users to navigate and find what they are looking for.

  • Contrast: Arngren has over 312 contrast errors according to Wave. It has bright text with a bright background making it difficult to read.
  • Repetition: The website lacks consistency in its design, with different fonts, colors, and layouts used throughout the site.
  • Alignment: The website has a chaotic layout, with elements placed haphazardly on the page.
  • Proximity: Related items are not grouped together, making it difficult for users to find what they are looking for. It is very cluttered and overwhelming.

Overall, Arngren is an example of a website that doesn't meet the CRAP principles and could benefit from a complete redesign to improve its usability and visual appeal.

Modrinth

Modrinth is a platform for discovering and sharing Minecraft mods. The website has a clean and modern design, with a focus on usability and user experience. The layout is simple and easy to navigate, with clear categories and filters to help users find the mods they are looking for.

  • Contrast: Modrinth has no contrast errors according to Wave. The text is easy to read with a good contrast ratio.
  • Repetition: The website uses a consistent color scheme, text, and layout throughout the site, creating a cohesive and professional look.
  • Alignment: The website has a well-organized layout, with elements aligned in a grid system that makes it easy to find information.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped together, making it easy for users to find what they are looking for. The use of whitespace also helps to create a clean and uncluttered look.

Overall, Modrinth is an example of a well-designed website that meets the CRAP principles and provides a good user experience. The website is easy to use and visually appealing, making it a great resource for Minecraft players looking for mods.
