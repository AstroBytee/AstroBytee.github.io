Awards & Honors

Larry has been recognized by the Governor of SC, the SC House of Representatives, Association of the US Army (AUSA), the Military Officers Association (MOAA), the Department of Veterans Affairs, the Ralph H. Johnson VA Medical Center (RHJVAMC), The Veterans Health Administrations Veterans Integrated Service Network (VISN) VII, Fisher House Foundation, Fisher House Charleston, Fisher House Columbia, the SC Civilian Assistant to the Secretary of the US Army (CASA), the American Legion, the Veterans of Foreign Wars (VFW), The National Association of Aging in Place, the US Army, two police departments, and others for his outstanding volunteer work and donations.
