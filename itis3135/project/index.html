About Larry Dandridge

W. Larry Dandridge is a Vietnam War veteran and an energetic, innovative, award-winning, and published writer, speaker, entrepreneur, leader, master logistician, analyst, business process re-engineering expert, newspaper and magazine writer, proposals manager, and Lean Six Sigma Black Belt.

Dandridge is the CEO of Tigers, Vikings, & Vipers (TVV) Publishing LLC. TVV Publishing LLC is a service-connected, disabled owned, small business, specializing in military history, police action, and children's books. The business also assists other businesses and government organizations to reinvent their operation through tools of business process, re-engineering, and Lean Six Sigma studies. TVV produces informative newspaper articles, books, business plans, quality and continuous improvement plans, procurement plans, strategic risk plans, and other plans.
