Project Overview

Overview:

  • Purpose: To provide veterans service organizations, the VA, state department of veterans affairs, customers, military members, veterans, friends, family, and others:
    1. Access to information on Tigers, Vikings, & Vipers (TV&V) Publishing Limited Liability Company (LLC) and Author W. Larry Dandridge's published works, books, articles, and contact information.
    2. The ability to order copies of Larry's Books, articles, and services. (Through 3rd Party Vendors such as Amazon, Barnes & Noble, etc.)
  • Intended Users: Veterans service organizations, veterans service officers, veterans, military members, the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), state department of veterans affairs, customers, military members, veterans, friends, family, and others
  • Overview of content: Picture of BLADES OF THUNDER (Book One), sample reviews of the book, copies of over 250 articles by Larry Dandridge on veterans' benefits, and over 20 articles on leadership, logistics, aircraft maintenance, law enforcement, what citizens should know about a police officer's use of force authority, aeronautics, hospice, end-of-life planning, and other subjects.
